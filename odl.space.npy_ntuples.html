npy_ntuples¶

CPU implementations of n-dimensional Cartesian spaces.

Classes

NumpyFn(size[, dtype]) Vector space F^n with vector multiplication.
NumpyFnArrayWeighting(array[, exponent, …]) Weighting of Fn by an array.
NumpyFnConstWeighting(constant[, exponent, …]) Weighting of NumpyFn by a constant.
NumpyFnCustomDist(dist) Class for handling a user-specified distance in NumpyFn.
NumpyFnCustomInner(inner[, dist_using_inner]) Class for handling a user-specified inner product in NumpyFn.
NumpyFnCustomNorm(norm) Class for handling a user-specified norm in NumpyFn.
NumpyFnMatrixWeighting(matrix[, exponent, …]) Matrix weighting for NumpyFn.
NumpyFnNoWeighting([exponent, dist_using_inner]) Weighting of NumpyFn with constant 1.
NumpyFnVector(space, data) Representation of an NumpyFn element.
NumpyNtuples(size, dtype) Set of n-tuples of arbitrary type.
NumpyNtuplesVector(space, data) Representation of an NumpyNtuples element.

Functions

npy_weighted_dist(weights[, exponent, use_inner]) Weighted distance on NumpyFn spaces as free function.
npy_weighted_inner(weights) Weighted inner product on NumpyFn spaces as free function.
npy_weighted_norm(weights[, exponent]) Weighted norm on NumpyFn spaces as free function.
