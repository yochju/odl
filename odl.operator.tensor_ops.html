tensor_ops¶

Operators defined for tensor fields.

Classes

MatrixOperator(matrix[, domain, range]) Matrix-vector multiplication as a linear operator.
PointwiseInner(vfspace, vecfield[, weighting]) Take the point-wise inner product with a given vector field.
PointwiseInnerAdjoint(sspace, vecfield[, …]) Adjoint of the point-wise inner product operator.
PointwiseInnerBase(adjoint, vfspace, vecfield) Base class for PointwiseInner and PointwiseInnerAdjoint.
PointwiseNorm(vfspace[, exponent, weighting]) Take the point-wise norm of a vector field.
PointwiseSum(vfspace[, weighting]) Take the point-wise sum of a vector field.
PointwiseTensorFieldOperator(domain, range, …) Abstract operator for point-wise tensor field manipulations.
