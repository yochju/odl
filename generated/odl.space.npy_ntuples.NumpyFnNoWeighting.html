NumpyFnNoWeighting¶

class odl.space.npy_ntuples.NumpyFnNoWeighting(exponent=2.0, dist_using_inner=False)[source]¶

Bases: odl.space.weighting.NoWeighting, odl.space.npy_ntuples.NumpyFnConstWeighting

Weighting of NumpyFn with constant 1.

For exponent 2.0, the unweighted inner product is defined as:

<a, b> := b^H a

with b^H standing for transposed complex conjugate.

For other exponents, only norm and dist are defined.

Attributes
